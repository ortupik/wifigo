Important Information

Your Wi-Fi access has been activated for multiple devices with the following benefits:

  • All devices share the same session simultaneously
  • Your data usage is shared across all devices
  • All devices can connect at the same time

How to Connect Your Other Devices

1 Connect this device first

This device will automatically connect to the Wi-Fi network when the countdown ends. You can also click the "Connect Now" button below.

2 On your other device(s), connect to the Wi-Fi network

Find and connect to the same Wi-Fi network as this device.

Network Name: The same Wi-Fi network you're currently trying to access.
3 Open a browser on your other device(s)

Open any website in your browser. You'll be automatically redirected to the login page.

4 Enter the phone number you used for payment

On the login page, enter the same phone number you used to pay for this service. The system will recognize that you've already paid for multiple devices.

Important: Use the exact same phone number format you used during payment.

Helpful Tips

  • Sessions are linked: All devices share the same internet session, so if one device logs out, all devices will be logged out.
  • Maximum devices: You can only connect the number of devices you paid for. Additional devices will require a separate purchase.
  • Need help? If you experience any issues connecting your devices, please contact our support team for assistance.
